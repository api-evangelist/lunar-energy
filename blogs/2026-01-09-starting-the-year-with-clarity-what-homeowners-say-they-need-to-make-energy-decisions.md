A survey of 75 homeowners reveals barriers to energy decision-making around solar, batteries, and heat pumps, particularly a confidence gap driven by unclear ROI comparisons and conflicting information rather than lack of capability. The research highlights the need for clearer, more credible guidance tailored to different decision-making styles.
