Lunar Energy partnered with Octopus Energy to offer Texas customers an integrated solution combining fixed-rate electricity pricing, a home battery system, and intelligent energy management without upfront hardware costs. The model is expected to expand to other regions as the companies work to enhance grid resilience through distributed home battery resources.
